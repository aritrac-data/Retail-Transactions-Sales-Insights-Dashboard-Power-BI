🔹 Key Revenue Growth Opportunities
Strong seasonal dependence, with Q4 generating the highest net revenue.


Certain categories exhibit high sales volume but lower revenue contribution.


Weekend sales consistently underperform compared to weekdays.


High discount levels at some stores do not result in proportional revenue growth.


🔹 Top 3 Business Recommendations
Focus on maximizing Q4 and peak-season performance through optimized inventory and targeted promotions.


Re-evaluate pricing and discount strategies for high-volume, low-revenue categories to improve margins.


Introduce weekend-specific campaigns to unlock additional revenue potential.
